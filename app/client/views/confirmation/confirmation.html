Transportation

HackSC will be providing buses to several schools in California. For logistical purposes, please let us know if you will be needing bus transportation to and from HackSC. If you plan on driving, please note that our venue is the California Market Center in Downtown Los Angeles (110 E 9th St, Los Angeles, CA 90079).

Please note that unfortunately, we cannot provide any travel reimbursement for flights or other modes of transportation.
